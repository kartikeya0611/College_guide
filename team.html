College Teams

Acceleracers

Group of students coming together to design, fabricate and market their creations in the field of automobile. Talent, enthusiasm, hard work, professionalism, etc. are some of the qualities that we have in abundance and which we carry with us every where we go. On the race track as well as in the world.

Robocon

MIT-WPU Tech Team is the official robotics team of MIT-WPU. It is known for its innovative solutions in the field of robotics and the unique way we promote robotics in the society. The team consists of highly enthusiastic and hard-working students, striving for excellence in the field of robotics.

Aarohan

Aarohan, is the annual cultural festival organized by the students of MIT WPU, Pune. It is held in the month of January.



Texephyr

Texephyr is the annual science and technology festival of Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune. Before 2014, there were various departmental events in the college. The event was founded as a common tech-fest for the entire college making it the first since 1983 when the college was established.

Sky Troopers

Team The Skytroopers , the official aeromodelling team of MIT-WPU,Pune believes in achieving what it aspires. Our first flight took off in 2017, in a small classroom with a bunch of few aero enthusiasts.We had the zeal and with everyone's support we created a team, with sheer determination we reached heights and were AIR 10th in SAEISS ADC in 2018 which was our first attempt. But, lack of awareness in India's large population about aeromodelling has driven our team to the dire need to educate and share knowledge across the country by any possible means.We take workshops at schools and colleges in remote and urban areas and have been successful in doing that ,but it takes a lot of effort and financial aid .To fulfill our vision of inculcating aeromodelling in younger minds and fabricating our creativity we require your support so that we both help develop our generation.

Sports Summit Team

This is the flagship event of MIT’s Sports Lab. It brings together a curated group of doers - those in industry and academia who have big visions and are on the forefront of their disciplines. It is an opportunity for all of our affiliates, supporters, and members of the MIT community to come to campus and share advances, challenges and passions that lie at the intersection of engineering and sports.
